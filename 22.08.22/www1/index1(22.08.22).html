Anthony de Mello - Looking for acceptance

Someone once put it beautifully: life is something that happens to us while we are busy making plans. We are busy impressing everyone around us, making sure we are well received. Let's talk about the addiction seeking acceptance!

Fryderyk Nietzsche: Defeat the dragon

The camel is an animal on which humans place many burdens, including the burden of lack of autonomy. A camel (as is a camel) goes with all this burden to the desert. What is the desert? It's a place where you are alone, where you can't hear all that "should".

Alan Watts: The need of control

I wonder what you would do if you had the power to dream any dream of your choice at night. And of course you would have the power to influence your perception of sleep time. You could, for example, live 75 years of subjective time within an eight-hour sleep.
